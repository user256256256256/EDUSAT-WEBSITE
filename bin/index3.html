Eurosat Group provides the highest quality in Enterprise Resource Planning systems and services to meet the demand of Academic Institutions today.

Who We Are

--%>

Edusat ERP, is a completely Web-based Enterprise Resource Planning system enabled with a Mobile App that uses a normal computer and Smart Phones to deliver the best combination of features, function and usability. , Edusat ERP is designed meticulously to meet the customized needs of Academic Institutions spanning from Primary to Advanced Levels. With a robust architecture and intuitive interface, Edusat ERP streamlines administrative processes, enhances academic management, and ensures financial transparency, empowering educational institutions to focus more on their core mission of delivering quality education.

Our Onboarding Process

  • Initial Consultation read more...
    • - Description: Schedule a consultation with our team to understand your school's unique needs and goals.
    • - Duration: 1-2 hours.
    • - Outcome: Customized onboarding plan tailored to your institution.
  • Setup and Configuration read more...
    • - Description: Our experts will assist in setting up Edusat, configuring modules to match your school’s requirements.
    • - Duration: 1 day.
    • - Outcome: Fully configured Edusat ERP system ready for use.
  • Data Migration read more...
    • - Description: Seamlessly migrate existing data (student records, financial data, etc.) to Edusat.
    • - Duration: 1 day.
    • - Outcome: All historical data accurately imported and verified.
  • Training Sessions read more...
    • - Description: Conduct comprehensive training sessions for administrators, teachers, and staff.
    • - Duration: 1-3 days.
    • - Outcome: Staff proficient in using Edusat's features and functionalities.
  • Pilot Testing read more...
    • - Description: Run a pilot test to ensure the system works smoothly and address any issues.
    • - Duration: 1 week.
    • - Outcome: Fine-tuned system with any necessary adjustments made.
  • Go Live read more...
    • - Description: Officially launch Edusat in your school with full support from our team.
    • - Duration: 1 day.
    • - Outcome: Edusat fully operational and in use by your school community.
